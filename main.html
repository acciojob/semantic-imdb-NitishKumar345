Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious. The film explores the themes of dreams, reality, and the power of the mind.

  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
